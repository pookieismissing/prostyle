Serving Downtown New Haven Since 1996

Thirty years on State Street. Octavious McAuley opened ProStyle in 1996 and hasn't stopped since. The shop runs on a simple idea: if you sit in the chair, you're getting his full attention, his best work, and a cut you can trust. No shortcuts, no compromises.

ProStyle is a one-chair operation. That means you're not getting passed off to whoever's available. You're getting Octavious. The fades are clean, the lines are sharp, and when you leave you look right. That's why clients keep coming back and that's why the shop holds a 4.9-star rating across 70+ Google reviews.

Whether it's a full shampoo and cut, a beard shape-up, a texturizer, or just an edge to hold you over, every service at ProStyle is built around what Octavious calls QE&P: Quality, Excellence, and Professionalism. It's not a slogan. It's the standard.
