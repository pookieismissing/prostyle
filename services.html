Services

ProStyle Haircutting Services · Service Menu
Quality, Excellence & Professionalism — Makes the Difference — Since 1996

Every service at ProStyle starts with a consultation. Octavious wants to see what you're working with and hear what you're going for before anything else. Bring a reference photo if you have one. If you don't, he'll walk you through the options.

Services are organized by what's included. The "Initiator" is your standard full haircut. The "Groomer" adds the beard and mustache. The "Sustainer" is for maintenance between cuts. And the "Looker" line covers chemical services like texturizers and color. Each service has an (A) and (B) option depending on whether you want a fade or an even-length cut. Read the details below or call (203) 865-2222 if you have questions about which service fits what you need.

Pricing is available when you book online or by phone.

The "Initiator"
A Full Shampoo & Haircut. The Beard & Mustache are *NOT included here.
(A) Service
  • A Shampoo (unless shampooed within the hour before arrival)
  • ANY Fade, blend, taper, or skin sides
  • A full scissor, precision haircut
  • Both the top and sides are serviced and shortened
  • A clipper edge and a warm razor edge (unless the razor is *not desired)
  • BEARD & MUSTACHE ARE NOT SERVICED.
*Shampoo is required and included in the "Full Haircut" cost.
(B) Service
  • Shampoo (unless shampooed within the hour of arrival)
  • *No Fade, taper, or blend
  • The top and side are even and the same length
  • Brush length
  • Buzz
  • Bald, shaved head
  • *Exception: Sides only, taper, fade, blend, or skinned sides. (*A shampoo & head top are NOT serviced with this option)
  • A clipper edge and a warm razor edge (unless the razor is *not desired)
  • BEARD & MUSTACHE ARE NOT SERVICED.
*Shampoo is required and included in the "Full Haircut" cost.

The "Sustainer"
ONLY an Edge, Outline Service. This is *NOT a Full Haircut service.
(A) Service — 3 features included
Beard & Mustache *included.
  • A clipper edge line and a warm razor edge (unless the razor is *not desired)
  • A light trim of uneven hairs. (*NOT a Full Haircut and no shortening of length)
  • Beard, mustache, or both are *included.
  • A Shampoo is optional and is *not required as with a Full Haircut.
(B) Service — 2 features included
Edge Outline, OR Beard and Mustache are serviced. *This is NOT a full haircut service.
  • *PLEASE NOTE: This service is a choice of numbers 2 or 3.
  • A clipper edge line and a warm razor (unless the razor is *not desired) & a light trim of uneven hairs. (*NOT a Full Haircut and no shortening of length).
  • A clipper edge line and a warm razor edge (unless the razor is *not desired) of the Beard, mustache, or both, & trimming/shaping the hair. (*Beards 1/2 inch or longer)
  • A Shampoo is optional and *not required as with a Full Haircut for number 2 of this service.
(C) Service — 1 feature included
ONLY an Edge, Outline, or other individual service. *This is NOT a full haircut service.
  • *PLEASE NOTE: This service is a choice of numbers 2, 3, or 4. This is *NOT a combined service.
  • The hairline clipper edge and a warm razor edge (unless the razor is *not desired). (this is ONLY an edge line)
  • Eyebrows *ONLY, by razor ($6 when included with a full haircut service)
  • The Beard, mustache, or both line. (this number is ONLY an edge line)
  • A Shampoo and conditioner service *ONLY
