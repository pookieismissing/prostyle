Meet Octavious McAuley

Since 1996, Octavious McAuley has been shaping more than hairlines in downtown New Haven. What started as a one-chair operation on State Street has grown into one of the city's most respected barbershops — built entirely on word of mouth, repeat clients, and a relentless commitment to doing the job right.

Every client who sits in the chair gets the same thing: Octavious's full attention, honest advice, and the kind of precision that only comes from thirty years behind the clippers. That's what QE&P — Quality, Excellence & Professionalism — looks like in practice.

ProStyle has been featured on WTNH and has earned a 4.9-star rating across 70+ Google reviews, but the real proof is in the chair. Clients keep coming back because the work speaks for itself.

How we do things at ProStyle

  1. 1 Book in advance to ensure a service is available when you need it. Walk-ins are welcome on Tuesdays (11am–3pm) and Saturdays (3–4pm sign-in, limited to 5 clients).
  2. 2 Cancel at least 12 hours before your appointment so no service opportunity is missed and another client can take the slot.
  3. 3 Communicate only through ProStyle's contacts — call (203) 865-2222 or email prostylecut@gmail.com — so no message is lost.
  4. 4 Take advantage of walk-in windows on Tuesdays (11am) and Saturdays (3pm) if an appointment isn't available.
